Signature Dishes

Royal Red Prawn & Saffron Rice

Grilled Carabinero, saffron rice emulsion, coral reduction, ñora oil

This dish centers on the Carabinero prawn, one of Spain's most prized seafood treasures. The prawn is lightly grilled, enhancing its natural aroma while keeping its tender, almost creamy texture.

The base is a saffron rice emulsion, crafted from a slow-cooked fish and bomba rice stock, strained and whisked with oil to create a silky, velvety texture.

Carabinero coral is reduced into a glossy, intense sauce that adds natural salinity and depth. The dish is finished with ñora-infused oil, providing subtle sweetness and signature Mediterranean aroma.

To elevate freshness and balance, it's garnished with micro sea greens and tiny preserved lemon accents, lending bright acidity without overpowering the prawn's delicate flavor.

Aged Ibérico & Smoked Bellota

Dry-aged Iberian pork loin, celeriac parmentier, pickled wild mushrooms, smoked bellota jus

This signature dish showcases the Iberian acorn-fed pork, one of Spain's most iconic ingredients. The loin is >dry-aged 10-14 days

It is charcoal-seared, creating a smoky, caramelized crust while preserving a juicy interior. Accompanying it is a creamy celeriac parmentier, rich and buttery, providing a subtle vegetal sweetness.

Wild mushrooms, gently pickled, introduce acidity and aromatic complexity. The dish is completed with a smoky jus made from Iberian pork bones, infused with oak wood and acorn shells to echo the flavors of the dehesa.

Finished with tender microgreens, herb oil, and a dusting of toasted rosemary, it delivers both aroma and elegance without masking the pork's intensity.
